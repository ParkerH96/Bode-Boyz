Free Programs and Tutorials

AVG

AVG is a great antivirus software. They have paid version but the free version does a really great job. Download this program, run the installer and scan your PC. Once scanned use the PC Tune Up to clean up and make your PC run at maximum performance

CCLEANER

CCLEANER is a great tool to clean your computer's junk files and remove unused programs with a breeze. This tool is great if your computer feels slugish and performance is lacking.

RougueKiller

RogueKiller is a security tool that can be used to terminate and remove malicious processes and programs from your computer. RogueKiller has the ability to remove infections such as ZeroAccess, TDSS, rogue anti-spyware programs, and Ransomwares.

Ninite

If you just bought a new computer, ninite is the easiest, fastest way to update or install software. Ninite downloads and installs programs automatically in the background.
