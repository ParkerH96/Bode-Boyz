Future Gaming Lounge


Currently we are in the process of gaining capital to start a gaming lounge near the campus of the University of Kentucky. In this gamming lounge there will be PC, Xbox and PS4 stations for customers to come pay for how many hours they would like to play. Another area that everyone will love are the TVs with couches that anyone can come in watch some Esports and chill. This will be a free area for everyone to enjoy.
