Zack Arnett

Co-Owner & CEO


Zack is an undergraduate student at the University of Kentucky studying Computer Engineering and Computer Science. The idea for Wildcat Gaming came to him when he was in Chicago going to a Rocket League LAN event that was held at a gaming lounge.He thought that if he had a place like this to go to when he was kid he would have loved it. Being an Entrepenuer, he decided that he could just start one himself. Bringing on Parker Householder and Madison Bartlett onto the team to gain help and skills where he was lacking.
